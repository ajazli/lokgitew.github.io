Welcome to Lok Gitew

Your Street Food Destination in Serpong

Lok Gitew brings the vibrant flavors of Singaporean and Malaysian street food to Hampton Promenade, Serpong, Tangerang. We're more than just a café – we're your go-to spot for authentic street food that hits different, whether it's a casual weekday lunch or a weekend night out.

Our concept takes traditional favorites from the streets of Singapore and Malaysia and serves them with creative twists, all in a cozy, welcoming atmosphere. Whether you're dining in or grabbing takeaway, we've got you covered with authentic flavors and quality ingredients.

Every dish is freshly prepared to order, ensuring you get the best quality and taste every single time.
